In the Warhammer 40,000 universe, the Imperium of Man has a rich and complex fictional history divided into various ages. Here are the main ages:

0. The Age of Terra

The time before M15 in the Warhammer 40,000 universe is referred to as “The Age of Terra”. This period encompasses the early history of humanity, starting from the prehistory of Earth (Terra) up to the point when humanity began to venture into space and colonize other planets.

1.The Dark Age of Technology (M15-M25):

Also known as the Age of Technology, this was a time of great scientific advancement and expansion for humanity. Human civilization reached its peak, colonizing the galaxy with the aid of advanced AI and powerful starships.

2.The Age of Strife (M25-M30):

A period of galactic turmoil and decline, marked by widespread war, civil strife, and the collapse of human interstellar civilization. The development of psykers and the emergence of Warp Storms made interstellar travel and communication nearly impossible.

3.The Great Crusade (M30-M31):

Led by the Emperor of Mankind, this was an era of reconquest and reunification. The Emperor created the Space Marine Legions to reclaim lost human worlds and to spread the Imperial Truth, a secular doctrine aiming to unify humanity under the Imperium.

4.The Horus Heresy (M31):

A galaxy-wide civil war that erupted when Warmaster Horus, the Emperor’s favored son, turned against him, leading half of the Space Marine Legions in rebellion. This conflict nearly destroyed the Imperium and culminated in the Emperor’s internment in the Golden Throne.

5.The Scouring (M31):

Following the Horus Heresy, loyalist forces purged the galaxy of the remaining traitor forces. This period involved widespread campaigns to eradicate the influence of Chaos and restore order to the Imperium.

6.The Age of Rebirth (M32-M35):

Also known as the “Reconstruction,” this era saw the rebuilding and consolidation of the Imperium. Efforts were made to strengthen the Adeptus Terra, the bureaucracy that governs the Imperium, and reestablish control over lost territories.

7.The Nova Terra Interregnum (M34-M36):

A time of division within the Imperium, where the segmentum known as Nova Terra declared independence, leading to a civil war that lasted several centuries.

8.The Age of Apostasy (M36):

Marked by extreme religious strife and corruption, this era saw the rise of the tyrannical High Lord Goge Vandire, who led a bloody reign known as the Reign of Blood. His rule ended with the intervention of the Adepta Sororitas and the reformation of the Ecclesiarchy.

9.The Age of Redemption (M37-M41):

A period of renewed faith and zealotry, characterized by massive crusades to reclaim lost worlds and purge heresy. It was a time of religious fervor and the strengthening of the Ecclesiarchy’s influence.

10.The Time of Ending (M41):

The current era in the Warhammer 40,000 timeline. It is marked by increasing Chaos incursions, xenos threats, and internal strife. The Imperium is under constant siege from all sides, struggling to survive against overwhelming odds.
