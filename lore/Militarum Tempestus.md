The **Militarum Tempestus**, also known as the **Ordo Tempestus** is a military organisation of the Imperium.

A sub-faction of the Administratum, it enjoys far greater autonomy than the Regiments of the Imperial Guard. The Ordo's ranks are primarily composed of Tempestus Scions and Commissars, but also include specialists from a variety of Imperial organisations. The Militarum Tempestus is known for its elite training and the quality of its soldiers, complimenting the overwhelming numbers of the Imperial Guard.
